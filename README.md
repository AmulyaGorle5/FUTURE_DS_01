# FUTURE_DS_01 — Superstore Sales Analysis

## About This Project
Business sales data analysis completed as part of the Future Interns Data Science Internship (Task 1).
Analyzed 9,994 rows of US retail sales data (2014–2017) using Microsoft Excel.

## Objective
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- How do sales change over time?
- Which categories or regions are most profitable?
- Where should the business focus to grow faster?

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset — Kaggle
9,994 rows | 21 columns | US retail orders 2014–2017

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel — PivotTables, Charts, Dashboard

## Key Findings
- Revenue grew 51% from $484,247 (2014) to $733,215 (2017)
- Tables generate $206,965 in sales but LOSE $17,725 in profit
- Technology drives 51% of total company profit
- West region generates nearly double the profit of Central region

## Skills Demonstrated
Data cleaning, KPI analysis, trend analysis, insight generation, business storytelling
